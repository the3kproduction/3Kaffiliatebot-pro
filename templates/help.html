Discord Setup

Easiest platform to start with!

Step 1: Create a Discord Server (if needed)

  • Open Discord and click "+" to add a server
  • Choose "Create My Own" ‚Üí "For me and my friends"
  • Name it something like "Affiliate Products"

Step 2: Create a Webhook

  • Right-click your server name ‚Üí "Server Settings"
  • Go to "Integrations" ‚Üí "Webhooks"
  • Click "New Webhook"
  • Choose a channel (create #affiliate-products if needed)
  • Copy the webhook URL
Example webhook URL: https://discord.com/api/webhooks/123456789/abc123def456

Step 3: Add to AffiliateBot Pro

  • Go to Platform Settings in your dashboard
  • Click "Configure Platforms"
  • Paste your webhook URL in the Discord field
  • Click "Save Discord"

Telegram Setup

Great for personal channels and groups!

Step 1: Create a Bot

  • Message @BotFather on Telegram
  • Send /newbot
  • Choose a name for your bot
  • Choose a username (must end in 'bot')
  • Copy the bot token
Example bot token: 123456789:ABCdefGHIjklMNOpqrsTUVwxyz123456789

Step 2: Get Your Chat ID

  • Start a chat with your bot
  • Send any message to your bot
  • Go to: https://api.telegram.org/bot[BOT_TOKEN]/getUpdates
  • Find your chat ID in the response
Note: For groups, add your bot to the group first, then get the group's chat ID (will be negative).

Slack Setup

Perfect for team workspaces!

Step 1: Create a Slack App

  • Go to api.slack.com/apps
  • Click "Create New App" ‚Üí "From scratch"
  • Name your app and select your workspace

Step 2: Get Bot Token

  • Go to "OAuth & Permissions"
  • Add these scopes: chat:write, files:write
  • Install the app to your workspace
  • Copy the "Bot User OAuth Token"
Example bot token format: xoxb-YOUR_WORKSPACE_ID-BOT_ID-TOKEN_STRING

Step 3: Get Channel ID

  • Right-click your channel in Slack
  • Choose "Copy link"
  • The channel ID is at the end of the URL

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an Amazon Associates account? +

Yes, you need an active Amazon Associates (affiliate) account to earn commissions. AffiliateBot Pro automates the posting, but you need your own affiliate account to earn money. Sign up at affiliate-program.amazon.com if you don't have one yet.

How much can I earn with affiliate marketing? +

Earnings vary greatly depending on your audience, niche, and posting strategy. Amazon affiliate rates range from 1-10% depending on product categories. With consistent posting and good platform engagement, users report earning anywhere from $50-$5000+ per month.

Is this allowed by platform terms of service? +

Always check each platform's terms of service. Most platforms allow affiliate marketing if you:

  • Disclose affiliate relationships clearly
  • Don't spam or over-post
  • Provide value to your audience
  • Follow posting frequency guidelines

AffiliateBot Pro includes built-in frequency limits to help you stay compliant.

Can I customize the post content? +

Currently, AffiliateBot Pro uses AI-generated content optimized for conversions. Future updates will include custom templates and messaging options. The AI creates engaging posts that typically perform better than generic product descriptions.

What's the difference between subscription tiers? +

Free: 2 posts/day, basic features, 1 AI promotion per 5 hours

Premium ($29/month): 8 posts/day, Amazon search, analytics, unlimited AI

Pro ($79/month): 24 posts/day, advanced features, priority support

All tiers include 7-day free trials!

How does the AI select products? +

Our AI considers multiple factors:

  • Product ratings and review count
  • Price points that convert well
  • Trending and seasonal items
  • Your posting history to avoid duplicates
  • Category performance metrics

This ensures you're always promoting products with the highest conversion potential.

Can I use this for multiple affiliate programs? +

Currently, AffiliateBot Pro focuses on Amazon Associates for the best user experience. We're planning to add support for other major affiliate programs like ClickBank, ShareASale, and CJ Affiliate in future updates.

Is my data secure? +

Yes! We use industry-standard security practices:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We never store your passwords
  • API tokens are securely encrypted
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • We never share your data with third parties
