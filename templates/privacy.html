🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, subscription tier, and payment information
  • Platform Credentials: API keys and tokens for third-party platforms you choose to connect
  • Product Data: Products you save, promote, and interact with
  • Usage Analytics: Performance metrics, click tracking, and campaign statistics
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing automated affiliate marketing services
  • Processing payments and managing subscriptions
  • Connecting to and posting on your authorized platforms
  • Tracking performance and generating analytics
  • Improving our service and developing new features
  • Communicating important service updates and support
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Platform credentials are stored securely and never shared
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe

4. Third-Party Platform Integration

When you connect third-party platforms, we:

  • Only access the permissions you explicitly grant
  • Use your credentials solely for authorized posting activities
  • Comply with each platform's terms of service and privacy policies
  • Allow you to revoke access at any time through your settings

5. Amazon Affiliate Program

As part of our affiliate marketing service:

  • We convert Amazon links to include your affiliate ID
  • We track clicks and conversions for analytics purposes
  • We comply with Amazon Associates Program policies
  • Commission data is processed according to Amazon's terms

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information except:

  • With your explicit consent for specific integrations
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality)

7. Your Rights and Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Revoke platform permissions at any time
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Port your data to another service

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve performance
  • Provide personalized recommendations

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • Required to provide our services
  • Necessary for legal or business purposes
  • You have not requested deletion

Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days, except where required by law.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during any international transfers.

11. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify users of material changes via email or platform notification.

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us through:

  • The support system in your dashboard
  • Email communication through the platform
  • The contact methods provided in your account
GDPR & CCPA Compliance: We are committed to protecting your privacy rights under applicable data protection laws including GDPR and CCPA.
